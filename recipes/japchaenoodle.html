Japchae Noodles Recipe

Description

Serve japchae noodles hot, chilled, or at room temperature. These Korean glass noodles are delicious with teriyaki chicken or short ribs.

Ingredients

  • ½ pound Korean dang myun noodles
  • 2 ½ teaspoons sesame oil, divided
  • 2 teaspoons white sugar
  • 2 carrots, cut into match-stick size pieces
  • 3 green onions cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds

Steps

  1. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rolling boil.
  2. Whisk together soy sauce and sugar in a small bowl; set aside.
  3. Heat vegetable oil in a skillet over medium-high heat.
  4. Stir in green onions and mushrooms; continue cooking and stirring for 30 seconds.
  5. Pour in soy sauce mixture, then add noodles.
  6. Remove from heat and toss with sesame seeds and remaining 1 1/2 teaspoons sesame oil.
